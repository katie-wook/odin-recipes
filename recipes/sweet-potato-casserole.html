Sweet Potato Casserole

Description

Sweet potato casserole is a classic Thanksgiving staple and is often made plain or with a marshmallow topping. But this recipe features a delectable pecan topping, showcasing two fall flavors that pair beautifully in this Thanksgiving dish.

With the contrasting textures of the soft, fluffy sweet potato and the hard, crunchy pecans, this dish will be the talk of the dining table. Expect to have this dish cleaned out by the end of the meal, and for any possible leftovers to be highly contested.
